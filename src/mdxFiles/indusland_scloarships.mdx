# Regarding the Programme

For eligible and distinguished students, the IndusInd Foundation is giving the IndusInd Foundation Scholarship 2023. 
Candidates with a strong academic background who have passed the 12th grade from an accredited board are eligible for the IndusInd Foundation. 
The focus of the Indusind Foundation is on the underprivileged members of society who cannot afford to attend school. 
students majoring in science, business, or the arts are eligible for this Merit cum Means scholarship.


# How would one apply?



# Eligibility
 - The candidate must be a citizen of India.
 - Students must have at least 80% in their 12th grade class to graduate.
 - For UG admission in B.A., B.Com., engineering, medicine, paramedicine, BCA, and BBA, one must have enrolled in a government college or its equivalent.
 - You are not eligible for this award if you graduated with your 12th grade before 2018.
