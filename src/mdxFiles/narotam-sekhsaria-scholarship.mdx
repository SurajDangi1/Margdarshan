# Regarding the Programme
The Narotam Sekhsaria Scholarships are a non-government programme that provides 100% merit-based awards to deserving Indian students who want to continue further education in India and abroad at prestigious institutions in Australia, the UK, and other nations. 
Based on the training, the Narotam Sekhsaria Foundation offers up to INR 2 million to a person.

 - It is a loan award that is given to exceptional Indian students and is interest-free.
 - The award is only open to citizens who are Indian residents.
 - Only students under 30 years old may apply for the award.
 - The scholarship is given to a select group of students who have a track record of excelling academically.


# How to apply ?
The Narotam Sekhsaria Scholarships Foundation encourages candidates to apply for post-graduation programmes in January of each academic year. There is a quick registration process to submit an application for the scholarship.

Step 1: Register online via the official website's registration interface.
Step 2: Each applicant's login information will be generated throughout the registration process. Using the email address and password set up during registration, the applicant must log in.
Step 3: The candidate must pay a 500 INR application fee. Demand Draughts made payable to the Narotam Sekhsaria Scholarship Foundation and Net Banking are the two accessible payment methods.

The applicant must login with the specified credentials after registering in order to apply and monitor the status of their application.

The deadline for applications expires in mid-March, and application costs are non-refundable; incomplete applications are also regarded as rejected.
