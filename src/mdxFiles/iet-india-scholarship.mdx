# Regarding the Programme
The IET India Scholarship Award is India's largest undergraduate engineering scholarship initiative. 
The 7th edition of the award programme, designed by the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET) to recognise and foster future engineering leaders in India,
will motivate undergraduate engineering students by awarding their originality, innovation, leadership, and brilliance. 
Engineering students from all years (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year) and specialisations will compete for a cumulative scholarship reward of INR 100,000. 


# How to apply ?

 - You will be redirected to the IET India Scholarship Award 2023 official registration page.
 - Fill out the registration form with all of your information and submit it. 
 - Log in using your registered email address and password once you've registered. 
 - Fill out the scholarship application with your personal information, academic information, professional activity, and co-curricular activities. 
 - Submit your application.




# Eligibility

 - Students in their first, second, third, or fourth year of a full-time normal undergraduate engineering course (in any subject) authorised by AICTE/UGC are eligible.
 - Applicants must have completed all standard credit courses in one sitting.
 - Applicants must have received at least 60% in aggregate or a CGPA of at least 6 on a 10-point scale in the previous semesters.
 - There is no upper age limit for candidates.
 - Note: Applications are available to all IET members and non-members, as well as all engineering fields accepted by the AICTE/UGC. In addition, lateral entry students who begin the B.Tech degree in the second year are eligible to apply.



# Documents
No documents are required to be attached at the time of application
