# Regarding the Programme
A component of Tata Realty and Infrastructure Limited's CSR initiative is the Tata Realty Scholarship for Girls.
This scholarship programme will aid female students who are enrolled in B.E., B.Tech., or bachelor of architecture programmes. 
Students will receive financial aid to further their education. Only academic costs may be covered with the scholarship money.
Candidates that are interested can submit their applications online. Only the vidyasaarathi portal has online applications. 

# How to apply ?
Step1 : You should go the Vidyasaarathi's official website to apply for the scholarship. 
Step2 : You may click the "Browse available schemes" option on the portal's home page.
Step3 : Click the Apply button after finding the "Tata Realty Renewal Scholarship for Girls Pursuing B.E./B.Tech. (2021-2022)" or "Tata Realty Renewal Scholarship for Girls Pursuing Bachelor of Architecture (2021-2022)" scheme on the newly opened page.
Step4 : When the login page loads, click the register now button.
Step5 : Registration application form will open where you have to enter the following details as required
              - Name
              - Mobile number
              - Email id
              - Password
              - Captcha code
Step6 : After reading the information provided in the link linked, select the appropriate box. 
Step7 : To submit the registration form, use the submit option.
Step8 : Enter the OTP to confirm your email and cellphone number. 
Step9 : Fill in the rest of asked details in the application form likewise
 - Father Name, 
 - DOB, 
 - Gender, 
 - Category etc.
Step10 : Upload only jpeg or png files for the papers.
Step11 : After carefully reviewing, select the submit option. You must retain a physical copy of the completed application on hand for future reference.

# Rewards
Scholarship for Female Engineering/Technology Students (2023) $10,000
Scholarship for Women Studying Architecture (2023)60000.00


# Eligibility

 # Tata Realty Renewal Scholarship for Female B.E./B.Tech Students.
   - The applicant must have at least 60% in their 12th grade in order to be admitted to either the B.C.E.-Bachelor of Civil Engineering or the B.E./B.Tech programme.
   - The candidate must be a woman.
   - The applicant's family's annual income should not exceed 5,000.

 # Scholarship for Women Pursuing a Bachelor of Architecture from Tata Realty
   - A minimum of 60% in the applicant's 12th grade grade is required. 
   - must be admitted to the Bachelor of Architecture programme.
   - The applicant must be a woman.
   - The applicant's family's annual income should not exceed 5 lakh.
