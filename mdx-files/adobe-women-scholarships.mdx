# Regarding the Programme

Female students are encouraged to apply for the Women in Technology Scholarship 2023 through Adobe India. For the academic year 2021–2022, students enrolled in a dual degree B.Tech/M.Tech programme or a B.Tech degree can apply. The fellowship seeks to identify outstanding female candidates in the fields of science and technology and offers them the chance to develop their intellectual capacities.

# Eligibility

The following are the eligibility requirements for the Women in Technology Scholarship 2023:

- The applicant must be a female student enrolled in undergraduate or graduate engineering programmes in 2023.
- Candidates who intend to enroll in full-time UG or PG programmes for the 2023 academic year are eligible to apply.
- Computer science, computer engineering, or a closely related technical discipline must be the applicant's major.
- Candidates need to have a strong academic history.
- An applicant cannot be considered for this fellowship if they have a close family member who works in Adobe research.

# Rewards

- The winners will receive a $10,000 one-time payment.
- The chosen individuals will get the chance to interview for an Adobe internship.
- The recipients of the awards will receive mentoring from a top technical executive of Adobe.
- Along with other additional advantages, the scholars will also receive a one-year subscription to the Creative Cloud.

# How to Apply?

- You can complete the application online.
- To apply online, go [here](https://www.adobe.com/in/lead/creativecloud/women-in-technology.html).
- Include all the necessary information.
- Applications must also include four essays, a CV, academic transcripts from your current and previous institutions, three references, and an optional 60-second video or multimedia entry outlining your desired profession.
