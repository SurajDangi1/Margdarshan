# Regarding the Programme

This NDF Scheme offers research fellowships to students in order to accept full-time, deserving researchers.
All individuals who desire to enrol in Ph.D. programmes at technical institutes or university departments that have received approval from the AICTE in order to conduct research in priority areas.

# Rewards

- stipend of INR 28,000 per month
- if no hostels are available, an accommodation allowance
- An annual contingency grant of INR 15,000

# How to apply ?

Apply online through official application page of National Doctoral Fellowship.

# Eligibility

The research researchers who are accepted into the AICTE Research Institute will be awarded the fellowship or contingency.
The National Nodal Centre will improve the procedure for choosing applicants in accordance with the requirements for awarding National Doctoral Fellowships.
As listed in Annexure-II, the Nodal Center's duties, functions, and obligations.

At both the B.E./B.Tech/B.Pharm and M.E./M.Tech/M.Pharm levels, the applicant must have earned a cumulative grade point average of 7.5 on a scale of 10 or an equal.
Along with the aforementioned, the candidate should have passed the GATE or GPAT within the previous five years.
Candidates must have a cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of at least 7.0 on a scale of 10 or an equivalent,
or have at least 75% of the available points for general candidates and 70% of the available points for candidates from the reserved categories.

The candidate must be younger than 30 as of July 31 of the application year; however, candidates from the SC/ST, female, and physically challenged categories will receive a 5-year age relaxation.
