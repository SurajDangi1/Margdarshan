# Regarding the Programme

AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls 2022-23 is an initiative for female students enrolled to the first or second year of a diploma/degree level study through lateral entry in any AICTE-approved institution of the year.
The initiative is being conducted by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) with the goal of assisting girls seeking technical education in their growth.
The shortlisted individuals would earn INR 50,000 each year of studies.
Specifically, students admitted to the first year have a maximum of three years and lateral entry students have a maximum of two years.
The scholarship will be offered in the form of a lump sum payment for college fees, computer purchases, stationery, books, equipment, software, and so on.

# How to apply ?

Eligible individuals can apply for the scholarship by completing the procedures below: Step 1: Click the 'Apply Now' button on the right.

Step 2: Go to the 'Apply now' and click the 'New Registration' button.
Step 3: Next, choose 'Click here for more Scholarship Schemes offered on NSP for AY 2022-23'.
Step 4: Carefully read the rules, choose the project, and click 'Continue'.
Step 5: Enter the applicant's name, date of birth, cellphone number, and email address, as well as the state of residence, scholarship category (Pre Matric), scheme type (scholarship scheme), and gender.
Step 6: Enter your bank information (bank name, IFSC code, and account number).
Step 7: As an identity detail, choose Aadhaar or a bank account number and click the 'Register' button.
Step 8: The cellphone number is validated, and an OTP is created.
Step 9: Now, log in with your OTP and complete the application form.
Step 10: Following the successful submission of the application form, an application ID and password will be provided for future reference.

# Eligibility

- To be eligible, an applicant must be a female student enrolled in the first year of a diploma/degree programme OR
- get accepted into the second year of a diploma/degree course
- through lateral admission into any of the AICTE-approved colleges of the year
- Have an annual family income of less than INR 8 lakh

# Rewards

INR 50,000 per annum

# Documents

- Cass 10 and 12 marksheets (if relevant)
- A legitimate income certificate issued by the state or territory government'
- Admission letter provided by the Directorate of Technical Education for enrollment in a degree/diploma programme
- Certificate provided by the Institute's Director/Principal/Head
- Tuition fee receipt
- Aadhaar-enabled bank account passbook containing the applicant's image, account number, and IFSC code.
- Caste certificate (for applicants who are SC, ST, or OBC)
- The Aadhaar card
- Parents must sign a declaration affirming the accuracy of the information on the application form.
