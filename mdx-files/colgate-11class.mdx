# Regarding the Programme

The Keep India Smiling Foundational Scholarship & Mentorship Programme seeks to give those
who are eligible and talented but may not have the financial means to follow their aspirations, a solid foundational support. Along with providing financial assistance,
this scholarship scheme will also prioritise providing the recipients with career mentoring and advice as needed.

In addition to the scholarships for schooling listed below, there are other awards for athletes and those who volunteer their time. The Keep India Smiling Foundational Scholarship and Mentorship Programme for Sportspersons and Individuals is available by clicking HERE.

# How to apply ?

# Eligibility

- Class 10 board exam must have been passed in 2022.
- must be enrolled in Class 11 in an Indian school that is acknowledged
- A minimum of 75% on the Class 10 board examinations is required.
- Family income should be no more than INR 5 lakhs per year.

# Rewards

-INR 20,000 annually for two years.

# Documents

- a picture the size of a passport
- Income Proof: Income certificate, BPL certificate, food security certificate, or any other document of income issued by a competent government authority.
- Valid ID proof: Either an Aadhaar card, driver's licence, voter ID card, or Pan card.
- Class 12 marksheet, a fee receipt, an admission letter, a college ID card, and a bona fide certificate (if applying for admission to a professional programme that has previously been completed).
- Results of the entrance exams (if applying for a job)
