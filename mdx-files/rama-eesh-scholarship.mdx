# Regarding the Programme
A CSR initiative called Rama Eesh Scholarship has been launched by the Rama Eesh charity foundation. 
The trust offering this scholarship will offer financial help to the chosen candidates. 
After graduating from high school, students who want to pursue an MBBS degree at an accredited Government College are eligible to apply. 
The vidyasaarathi portal is where eligible applicants may submit their applications. The process of filling out the application form has already begun.
 After carefully reviewing the material, you can submit the application by the deadline.

# How to apply ?
 - Visit the vidyasaarathi portal's official website in order to apply for this scholarship.
 - You must locate and select the "browse available schemes" option on the home page.
 - There will be a new page that you must search for the "Rama Eesh Scholarship 2021â€“22" on. 
 - To access the login window, select the "Apply" option that is provided right below. 

# Rewards
Beneficiaries who are chosen for this scholarship will receive a 50000 rupee financial help package. 
The beneficiary's bank account will receive the funds from the Rama Eesh charity trust immediately.



# Eligibility
 - Candidates must hold a 12th-grade passing certificate from a school that has been approved by the CBSE or state board with at least 50% of the required marks.
 - The applicant must have enrolled in a 4.5-year, full-time MBBS programme at any Himachal Pradesh government college.
 - The candidate must be an Indian national who lives continuously in Himachal Pradesh.
 - The candidate's family's annual income cannot exceed INR 800,000.
