# Regarding the Programme
Junior Research Fellowship 2022 at IIT Kharagpur Department of Computer Science and Engineering is a research opportunity for B.Tech. degree holders.
 The fellow will be working on the project "Development of Algorithms and Tools for Log Analytics and Vulnerability Assessment (ALV)." The selected fellow would get a monthly stipend of up to INR 31,000, based on qualifications and experience.



# How to apply ?
The eligible candidates can apply for the fellowship by using the below-mentioned steps - 
Step 1: Click on the "Apply Now" button and read the details.
Step 2: Sign up (New User for the selective advertisement) and log in with the Login ID, password, and office order no.
Step 3: Fill up the profile, academic, experience details and upload a scanned passport size photo and signature and finally Freeze the application.
Step 4: After you've frozen the web application, create a PDF document. 

# Rewards
A stipend of up to INR 31,000 per month, based on qualifications and experience, will be given to the chosen fellow.


# Eligibility

An applicant must meet the following requirements in order to be considered:
 - You must be 28 years old.
 - Have a B.Tech. in CSE, ECE, or a similar field.
 - Possess a valid GATE qualification
