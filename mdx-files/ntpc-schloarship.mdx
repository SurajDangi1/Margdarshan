# Regarding the Programme
The National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC) is providing a scholarship programme for students in the deserving SC, ST,
and PC categories who are enrolled full-time in engineering degree programmes.
Students enrolled in engineering programmes at IITs, NITs, and BITs in the fields of electrical, instrumentation, mechanical, computer science, electronics, and telecommunication are eligible for the NTPC Scholarship Scheme 2023.
A total of 35 scholarships will be awarded to deserving students, of which 20 will go to students who fall under the SC category, 
10 to those who fall under the ST category, and 5 to students who are physically challenged.


# How to apply ?
- Application submission will take place offline.
 - Candidates can get an application form from the institution's head or another designated representative.
 - On www.ntpc.co.in, an application form is also available for download.

# Rewards
For the length of the programme, recipients of the NTPC scholarship will earn INR 1500 each month.


# Eligibility
 - A applicant must be enrolled in the second year of a B.E. or B.Tech programme at one of the institutions listed.
 - The applicant's age should be no more than forty years old.
 - He or she must fall into the SC, ST, and PC categories.
 - Rajasthan, Delhi, J&K, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Chandigarh, and Punjab should be the candidate's home states.
 - A candidate cannot be receiving several forms of financial aid.
